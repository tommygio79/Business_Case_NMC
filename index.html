Okadoc Case Study – NMC Healthcare

✨ Deployment Year: 2023
🏥 Industry: Healthcare
🏦 Sector: Private
👨‍⚕️ Doctors: 1,800+
🇦🇪 Territory: UAE

🚫 Challenges

NMC Healthcare, the UAE’s largest private healthcare provider, struggled with high no-show rates and outdated admin systems. Long wait times and inefficient follow-ups hindered growth and patient satisfaction across its extensive network.

🔧 Services Provided

  • Patient Experience
  • Strategy & Digital Transformation
  • IT Development & Engineering
  • Telehealth
  • Omnichannel Communication
  • Training & Onboarding
  • Change Management

🚀 Solutions Deployed

  • Patient Engagement Platform
  • Hospital Efficiency Platform
  • Digital Front Door
  • Video Consultation Platform
  • HIS/EMR Integration
  • Mobile Apps

🌟 Outcomes

Okadoc’s platform helped NMC significantly reduce no-shows and wait times, streamline admin processes, and extend access via telehealth. Patients can now manage appointments flexibly, while staff are freed from redundant tasks. This transformation positions NMC as a digital leader in smart, patient-centered healthcare.
